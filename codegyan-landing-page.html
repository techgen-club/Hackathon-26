About
CodeGyaan

01 / EVENT OVERVIEW

CCodeGyaan is an inter-collegiate hackathon that brings together passionate innovators, developers, designers, and problem-solvers to create impactful technology solutions for real-world challenges.

Centered around the theme "AI for Inclusive Digital Transformation", the hackathon encourages participants to harness Artificial Intelligence to build solutions that are accessible, ethical, sustainable, and socially impactful.

Over three exciting days, participants will collaborate, innovate, and transform ideas into functional prototypes while receiving mentorship from experts and presenting their solutions before an esteemed panel of judges.

A hybrid hackathon for real-world innovation.
