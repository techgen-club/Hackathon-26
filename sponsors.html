Why partner with CodeGyaan

CodeGyaan is an inter-collegiate hackathon that brings together passionate innovators, developers, designers, and problem-solvers to create impactful technology solutions for real-world challenges.

Centered around the theme "AI for Inclusive Digital Transformation", the hackathon encourages participants to harness Artificial Intelligence to build solutions that are accessible, ethical, sustainable, and socially impactful.

Connect directly with future talent, amplify your brand, and help shape a premium student experience.

Purpose of the Partnership

Why partner

As part of our efforts to provide enriching learning experiences and industry exposure to students, we are seeking sponsorship and partnership support from organizations that share our commitment to innovation, technology, and education.

By partnering with TechGen Club, organizations will have the opportunity to connect with a dynamic student community, enhance their brand visibility, and contribute to initiatives that empower the next generation of technology professionals.
